About Me

I'm Sreesanth — from Vizag, currently at VIT-AP studying Computer Science. I've been to six schools across Andhra Pradesh, each one leaving something permanent.

The most formative was Sainik School Kalikiri, near Chittoor — one year of military structure, early mornings, and strangers becoming brothers. It changed how I show up in every room since.

Now I'm in my second semester, learning Java and data structures, building this site, and tracking everything publicly. The goal isn't perfection — it's consistency.
